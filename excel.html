Customer Attrition & Engagement Risk Dashboard

An Excel-based banking analytics project analysing how digital engagement, product ownership, and transaction behaviour drive customer churn and revenue risk across a simulated retail banking portfolio of 10,000 customers.

Retail Banking Loan Portfolio Risk & Performance Analysis

An advanced Excel modelling project analysing a simulated retail banking loan portfolio of 2,000 loan records across multiple branches, products, and borrower profiles — evaluating credit risk exposure, lending concentration, collateral coverage, and portfolio yield.

Key Insight

Mortgage loans dominate at 73% of total exposure. Nyeri branch holds the largest portfolio at ~KES 1.9B. Karen and Kisumu show the highest default exposure — indicating concentrated credit risk in those regions.
